About the journal

The Mangalayatan Journal of Scientific and Industrial Research (MJSIR) is a peer-reviewed journal dedicated to advancing knowledge in the fields of Advanced Engineering, Science, and Pharmacy. Launched under the aegis of Mangalayatan University, Aligarh, MJSIR seeks to create a dynamic platform where researchers, academicians, and professionals from across the globe can contribute their latest research findings, innovative ideas, and practical insights. The journal aims to publish high-quality, original research that contributes to the growth of knowledge in diverse areas such as mechanical and electrical engineering, biotechnology, pharmaceutical science, chemical engineering, and industrial applications. By fostering cross-disciplinary dialogue and encouraging the sharing of new methodologies, MJSIR intends to bridge the gap between theory and practice, facilitating scholarly debate and innovation in scientific and industrial domains. .
